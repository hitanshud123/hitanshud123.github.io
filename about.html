My Hub is a tool built by three first-year post-secondary students from the University of Waterloo. The tool is designed to act as a “hub” for online learning, where students can customize a dashboard however they want. Users can keep the links of all of the resources and platforms for online learning on the dashboard so that they are organized with their online education. The customizability of the dashboard allows the user to create the dashboard that fits their aesthetic, effectively improving the user experience. With a combination of Photos, Notes, and customized links, it’s certain that the user will increase their productivity.
